Who we are

Built on Craft

Kadmia Constructions is a dynamic and innovative custom home builder with over 10 years of experience delivering high-quality homes across the Greater Sydney Region.

Our philosophy is simple: every home should be tailored, unique, and built to stand the test of time. We're there every step of the way — from concept and design, right through to delivery and handover.

Our Promise
We don't just build houses — we create homes that reflect the people who live in them. High quality, personalised from first consultation to final handover.
Our Commitment
Every client is unique. Whether a growing family or a savvy investor, we deliver a tailor-made approach — on time, on budget, defect free.
Our Mission
To bring your vision to life. We believe every project starts with an idea, and it's our role to transform that idea into a home you'll be proud to call your own.
